FAQ

The best time to visit Skardu is from April to October when the weather is pleasant and all routes are open.

Transportation is a major concern, especially about flight vs road travel. Short Answer: You can fly (1 hour from Islamabad) or drive via the Karakoram Highway (20–24 hours).

Travelers want a ready list of must-see attractions.Short Answer: Shangrila Resort, Upper Kachura Lake, Shigar Fort, Deosai, and Manthoka Waterfall.

Many people ask whether they can go independently or need a tour.Short Answer: You can travel independently, but tour companies make the journey smoother with transport, hotels, and local knowledge included.

Yes, most parts of Skardu have 4G coverage, especially with Zong and SCOM networks. Some remote valleys may have limited or no signal, so plan accordingly.
