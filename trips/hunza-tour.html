Overview

Experience the unmatched beauty of Hunza Valley with our 5 Days 6 Nights Hunza Tour. This journey takes you through majestic mountains, historic forts, turquoise lakes, and vibrant local culture. Perfect for families, couples, or adventure lovers, this tour combines natural beauty with cultural richness. From Karimabad to Khunjerab Pass, each day is filled with scenic wonder. Whether it’s spring blossoms or golden autumn, Hunza shines in every season. Discover why Hunza is called the “Heaven on Earth.”

Tour HighLights
  • Drive along the world-famous Karakoram Highway with breathtaking views
  • Visit Altit and Baltit Forts – ancient symbols of Hunza's heritage
  • Explore Attabad Lake and enjoy boating in crystal-clear waters
  • Passu Cones and Suspension Bridge – perfect for photography lovers
  • Day trip to Khunjerab Pass – the world’s highest border crossing
  • Cultural experience in traditional villages like Gulmit and Karimabad
  • Enjoy local cuisine, organic food, and stunning mountain landscapes

Tour Plan

We begin the journey early morning from Islamabad via the Karakoram Highway. Enjoy views of the mighty Indus River and Nanga Parbat en route. Reach Chilas by evening for an overnight stay.
After breakfast, we drive towards Hunza Valley passing through stunning mountain landscapes and the junction of three great mountain ranges. Arrive at Karimabad, check in to the hotel, and explore the local bazaar.
Begin the day with a visit to the historic Baltit Fort and Altit Fort, two architectural treasures of Hunza. In the evening, head up to Duikar (Eagle’s Nest) for breathtaking sunset views over Ultar Sar and Ladyfinger peaks.
Full-day excursion towards the upper Hunza region. Visit Attabad Lake for boating, then stop by the iconic Passu Cones and Passu Glacier. Continue to Khunjerab Pass (Pakistan-China border) – the highest point on the Karakoram Highway.
After breakfast, we begin the return journey. Depending on weather and road conditions, we’ll either travel via Naran (Babusar Pass) or return through Chilas and stay overnight in Naran or Besham.
On the final day, we head back to Islamabad with unforgettable memories of the Hunza Valley. Guests are dropped off at the agreed point in Islamabad by late afternoon.
