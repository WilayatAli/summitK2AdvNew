Overview

Explore the breathtaking beauty of Skardu in this 5-day tour featuring majestic mountains, pristine lakes, and rich Balti culture. Discover top attractions like Shigar Fort, Manthoka Waterfall, and the iconic Shangrila Resort. Perfect for nature lovers and cultural explorers alike.

Tour HighLights
  • Explore Skardu Valley – the gateway to the Karakoram mountains
  • Visit the historic Shigar Fort – a blend of Tibetan and Mughal architecture
  • Discover the scenic beauty of Shangrila Resort and Kachura Lakes
  • Witness the powerful Manthoka Waterfall in Kharmang Valley
  • Experience the unique Sarfaranga Cold Desert – the only one at high altitude
  • Enjoy breathtaking drives through lush valleys and rugged peaks
  • Capture stunning views and experience authentic Balti culture and hospitality

Tour Plan

Early morning departure from Islamabad via flight or drive through the scenic mountain highways. Upon arrival in Skardu, transfer to the hotel for rest and a brief visit to the local bazaar. Overnight stay in Skardu.
After breakfast, we explore the beautiful Shigar Valley. Visit the historical Shigar Fort and enjoy a walk along the riverbanks. Later, experience the Sarfaranga Cold Desert – perfect for photography and a unique high-altitude desert experience. Return to Skardu for the night.
Today we head out to Kharmang Valley to witness the majestic Manthoka Waterfall. Enjoy the peaceful natural surroundings and lunch near the waterfall. Return to Skardu by evening and relax at the hotel.
Visit the picturesque Shangrila Resort, also known as “Heaven on Earth.” Then hike or drive to Upper Kachura Lake – known for its deep blue waters and serene atmosphere. Enjoy boating and nature walks before returning to Skardu.
After breakfast, check out from the hotel. Transfer to Skardu Airport for flight or begin the road journey back to Islamabad. End of a memorable Skardu tour with unforgettable views and experiences.
